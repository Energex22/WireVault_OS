WireVault Alpha 1.2 — Vault Search Prototype

Required baseline: Alpha 0.9.6.2

1. Copy all patch contents into your WireVault_OS repository root.
2. Replace index.html.
3. Restart WireVault Core.
4. Close old WireVault tabs and open a new one.
5. Press Ctrl + Space.

Vault Search rises from the bottom and leaves the top navigation visible.
It does not replace or modify the normal menus.

Keyboard:
- Ctrl + Space: open or close
- Up/Down: choose result
- Enter: open result
- Escape: close

Controller prototype:
- Hold Start for about 0.65 seconds
